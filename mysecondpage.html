The Witcher Stories: The Last Wish

By Andrzej Sapkowski

The Witcher is a fantasy series of short stories and novels about the witcher geralt of rivia. in this book "witchers" are monster hunter who develop supernatural abilities (with training and body modification) at a young age to battle deadly beasts. The book have been adapted into a film, a television series, video games, and graphic novel series. The series of novel known as witcher saga. The short stories and novel have been translated into numerous languages, including English
